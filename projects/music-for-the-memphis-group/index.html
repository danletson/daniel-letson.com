Project

Music for the Memphis Group

Branding / Development / Personal

"Music for the Memphis Group" is a conceptual album of compositions by artist Matt Wellins, inspired by the post-modern Memphis design movement. In collaboration with the artist, I designed and built a unique website to present the audio alongside eye-popping graphics. A custom audio player and bold tiled backgrounds were designed to pay homage to the Memphis Group's aggressively kitschy graphic style. Use of perfectly-tiling SVG patterns and randomized transformations yielded an infinite number of thematic variations.
